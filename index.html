Prototypes

VR Physics Gun

This prototype was my first tests at creating a VR game. The idea was to recreate the physics gun from Garry's Mod if you haven't played Garry's mod the physics gun is a sort of weapon which has the power to pick up and move items at the end of its ray. In this prototype, I used a primitive cube as the weapon which could be held and thrown in any hand then when you press the trigger a small straight laser would show and at the end of it you could pick up items if they had a certain script on them. As you can see in the video items wouldn't move fixed with the end of the ray, that's how I wanted as that's the same as it is in Garry's mod, however, I couldn't manage to make a ray render so I had to stick to my straight line laser. This prototype just took a day to create and used the SteamVr Plugin for Unity and the primitive cubes for the assets.
